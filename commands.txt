Navigation :-
shift key + 'c' :- Selects input bar (works only on desktop)
enter key :- Executes function
space bar :- Scroll down by a page
shift key + space bar :- scroll up by a page

All commands and spells :-

'help' :- bring up help page
'lumos' :- light mode
'nox' :- dark mode
'accio *' :- Replace * with name of a page or blog number (0/featured for featured blog)
'cd *' :- Replace * with name of a page
'cat *' :- Replace * with blog number (0/featured for featured blog)
'cd' or 'cd ~' or 'cd /' :- Go back to home page (Equivalent to pressing home on the navbar)
'cd -' or 'cd ..' :- Go back one page (equivalent to pressing the back button in the browser)
'instagram' , 'twitter', 'linkedin', 'mail', 'github' :- bring us corresponding social porfiles
'rtd' : New Random Featured Article/Project (Throwback to Roll The Dice from CS 1.6)
